Introduction to Research

Research is an essential part of creating a well-constructed paper. This section provides an overview of research strategies and how to access relevant resources.

Steps for Effective Research

  1. Identify your research topic and formulate key questions.
  2. Gather information using academic databases and other resources.
  3. Organize your findings and create a structure for your paper.
